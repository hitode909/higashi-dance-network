薄萌葱（Usu-moegi）

Arashiyama Bamboo Forest
Arashiyama is a district on the western outskirts of Kyoto. You can find a Usu-moegi color in the bamboo tunnel here.

櫨染（Hajizome）

Kinkakuji (Golden Pavilion)
Hajizome is a color which means a power. Ashikaga Yoshimitsu (the 3rd shogun of the Ashikaga shogunate who ruled from 1368 to 1394 during the Muromachi period) built Kinkakuji. Its top two floors are completely covered in gold leaf.
